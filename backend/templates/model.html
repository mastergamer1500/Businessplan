Transparent Threat Analytics Engine rules

Pulse Check Neo ranks business stability based on dynamic danger calculations. Anyone can parse how our model thinks by matching simple financial limits with code conditions.

Algorithmic Vector Math Summary

The platform computes business exposure indices by directly dividing active deficits over overall cash assets.

• R = Money Coming In (Gross Revenue Inflows)
• E = Money Going Out (Operational Burn Costs)
• C = Total Bank Balance (Cash Reserve Liquidity Pool)
UNDERLYING MATHEMATICAL MODEL STRUCT
Danger Index = ( (E - R) / C ) × 100
Note: If your business income is higher than your costs, the calculation drops below zero, causing the application framework to mark your venture safe.
